About StudentHub

A smart and accessible student management portal for modern education.

🎓 Our Mission

StudentHub aims to provide students with a simple digital platform where they can manage academic information, events, profiles, and communication easily.

🌟 Our Vision

To create an efficient and accessible learning environment by connecting students, teachers, and administrators through technology.

🚀 Our Features

  • Student Dashboard
  • Online Registration
  • Event Management
  • Profile Management
  • Feedback System

Why StudentHub?

StudentHub is designed using HTML5 semantic elements and accessibility principles. It provides clean navigation, readable content structure, and user-friendly pages for every student.
